## PAPER 2 — TIMING

**Title:** Comparison of DSB1 and LuAG:Ce wavelength shifters for precision EM-shower timing at shower maximum in an ultra-compact W/LYSO:Ce sampling calorimeter (RADiCAL), 25-150 GeV

**Thesis:** Light yield - not WLS species - GOVERNS shower-max timing. Across four builds the stochastic term a of sigma_t = a/sqrtE (+) b tracks detected light (x2.3: DSB1 a=201 to LuAG a=455) while the floor b ~ 20 ps is shared among LYSO builds; the in-module MIXED comparison (same showers, per-capillary DSB1/LuAG ratio 0.99) resolves the LuAG-kinetics confound with no cross-build systematics; the LG-anchored saturation-recovered CFD (srCFD, ne lgcfd) is the ENABLING method (+3.1 ps at 150 GeV, floor 22.1->19.5 ps, demonstrated removal of the published 0.2 ns satellite), and the floor CONFIRMS the published 17.5 ps as shower-depth geometry (sigma_z = v_fiber*sigma_t ~ 1 X0). Headline is always the MEASURED 25.3 ps at 150 GeV; this paper delivers the parent's Sec 7.2 promise in 25 GeV steps.
