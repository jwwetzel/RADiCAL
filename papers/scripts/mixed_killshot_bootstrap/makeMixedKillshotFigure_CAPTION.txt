"Same-module, same-shower comparison of the two wavelength-shifter species in the MIXED RADiCAL
module (corner map confirmed by amplitude-independent pulse-shape discriminants). (A) Per-capillary
timing widths versus beam energy for the DSB1 (NE, SW) and LuAG:Ce (NW, SE) capillary pairs under

widths agree at the 10-20% level under the primary srCFD estimator; the observed spread across
energies dominates the statistical bootstrap uncertainty, giving 1.04 +/- 0.05. (C) Estimator
dependence: srCFD is the headline because it is least sensitive to clipping and fixed-threshold
walk; cfd05 (clip-walk penalizes the saturating DSB1 corners) and LED (noise penalizes the dim
LuAG corners) are diagnostic estimators. Set against the x2.3 cross-build stochastic-term ratio,
this same-shower near-parity disfavors intrinsic WLS re-emission kinetics as the dominant cause of
the cross-build timing difference, within this geometry and at these light levels."
