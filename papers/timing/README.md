# Paper 1 — Timing (RADiCAL, NIM A / elsarticle)

Paper 1 of the two-paper series from the May-2023 dataset (series index: `../README.md`).
**Status: at internal coauthor circulation.** Thesis: timing performance is governed primarily
by detected light yield, module response, and estimator choice rather than by WLS material
identity alone (same-shower MIXED control 1.04 ± 0.05 vs the >2× cross-build stochastic term).
