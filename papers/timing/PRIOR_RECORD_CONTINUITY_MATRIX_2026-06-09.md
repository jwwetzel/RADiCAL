## Executive verdict

**Circulate after minor edits.** The thesis, the headline numbers (25.7±0.6 ps; a = 203±6 vs 440±18; floor 18.8±0.8 confirming 17.5; MIXED 1.04±0.05), the same-shower control, and the claims-law compliance of every core claim survived all six audits intact — nothing requires reopening analysis, and the retired 0.99 MIXED ratio was verified never-public, so its exposure is fully handled by the existing circulation note. However, seven surgical defects must land before the PDF goes to coauthors, because each one would be caught by a parent-paper coauthor on first read: one violation of the manuscript's own claims law (the full-fiducial companion to the brightest-1000 headline is absent everywhere — grep-verified), two misstatements of the parent's published record (the cfd05 attribution and the 0.2 ns satellite cross-reference, both contradicted by the parent's own text as established independently by four auditors), two apparatus misstatements (LG digitisation quoted at 5 GS/s vs the author-confirmed 1 GS/s channel map; "16 SiPMs" contradicting the paper's own "8 SiPM channels"), one quantitative mislabel (the stochastic-term ratio 2.2 called a "light deficit" when the measured light ratio is ~3x), and one omitted applied selection (the Pb-glass containment veto, verified present in the production SelectionCuts.h). All are wording-level fixes plus two one-line verifications with coauthors; none touches a gated number.
