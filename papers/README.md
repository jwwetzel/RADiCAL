# RADiCAL papers — CERN SPS May 2023 dataset

Two papers from this one dataset, both building on the published parent paper
**NIM A 1068 (2024) 169737** (arXiv:2401.01747) and covering the roadmap in its Sec. 7.

**Series numbering, declared once:** the timing paper is **Paper 1** of this two-paper series.
(Some internal campaign documents — `memory_paper2_timing.md`, panel JSONs — refer to it as
"Paper 2", counting the parent NIM A publication as paper one of the programme. Both names
denote the same manuscript: `timing/radical_timing.tex`. The series numbering in this file is
authoritative for the repo.)

| Folder | Paper | Observable | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| [`timing/`](timing/) | **Paper 1 — Timing** | four-build σ_t(E), srCFD recovery, same-shower MIXED control, systematics, depth drift | **at internal coauthor circulation** |
| [`energy_position/`](energy_position/) | **Paper 2 — Energy + Position (+4D)** | shower-max energy (E_SM), transverse position, 4D demo | skeleton |

- **Paper 1 thesis:** timing performance is governed primarily by detected light yield, module
  response, and estimator choice rather than by WLS material identity alone — the same-shower
  MIXED control (σ_DSB1/σ_LuAG = 1.04 ± 0.05) set against the >2× cross-build stochastic-term
  difference. Delivers the parent's Sec. 7 item 2 promise on the parent's own pre-registered
  dataset. Headline: 25.7 ± 0.6 ps (brightest-1000, 150 GeV; ≈50 ps full fiducial).
- **Paper 2 thesis:** energy and transverse position from the same shower-max signals + a
  simultaneous (x, y, E_SM, t) demonstration. Localization analysis gated (GATE 2 reconciled);
  manuscript to build.

## Conventions (both papers)

- LaTeX: `elsarticle` (NIM A), two-column; build with `tectonic <main>.tex` inside the paper
  folder. The class + `orcidlink.sty` are vendored for editor portability; tectonic and
  Texifier/TexpadTeX both build. `\graphicspath{{figs/}}`.
- Figures: generated by the gate macros under `scripts/` (plus two `analyze/studies/`
  narrative macros for Figs. 2–4); manuscript copies live in `timing/figs/` and are inventoried
  with checksums in `timing/figs/MANIFEST.md`. Paper convention: no internal super-titles —
  captions carry the titles.
- Numbers: every quoted value obeys `memory_claims_and_forbidden_language.md` and traces per
  the provenance table in the repo-root `ANALYSIS_GUIDE.md`.
