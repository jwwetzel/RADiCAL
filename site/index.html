Research & Development of Optical Materials

Ultra-compact, fast-timing EM calorimetry

A radiation-hard R&D program timing electromagnetic showers to tens of picoseconds at shower maximum — tungsten / LYSO:Ce sampling modules the size of a human index finger, read out through wavelength-shifting capillaries. CERN SPS test beam, May 2023.

25.7 ± 0.6 ps
brightest 150 GeV showers at shower max (≈50 ps full fiducial)
18.8 ± 0.8 ps
fitted floor — confirms the published 17.5 ps
